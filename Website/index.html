Features


Real-Time Crop Monitoring

Visual dashboard showing real-time data on soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and crop health.

Benefit: Farmers can monitor their fields remotely, ensuring optimal growing conditions at all times.

Automated Irrigation Control

Smart irrigation system connected to the Agribot that automates watering based on soil moisture levels.

Benefit: Saves water and ensures crops receive the right amount of hydration, promoting sustainable farming practices.

Soil and Weather Analytics

Detailed analytics on soil conditions and weather forecasts, with AI-driven recommendations for optimal planting and harvesting.

Benefit: Helps farmers make data-driven decisions, improving crop yields and reducing resource waste.
